Who We Are

Family-Owned Since 1998 — Built on Trust

PeakShield Roofing was founded in Texas by master roofer Dale Harmon with a simple belief: every homeowner deserves an honest contractor who shows up, does the work, and stands behind it.

Today we operate across 12 states with over 85 certified crew members — but our family culture and commitment to quality have never wavered.

  • GAF Master Elite® Certified Contractor — top 3% nationwide
  • Fully licensed, bonded, and insured in every state we operate
  • 25-year workmanship warranty on all new installations
  • Transparent, itemized estimates — no hidden fees, ever
  • OSHA-compliant safety practices on every job site
